What's up with
Persona Fest 2023?

Biggest techno-cultural event of MIT ADT University. The fest is a celebration of creativity and innovation, and it brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals for a weekend of fun and learning. The event features live music performances, workshops, competitions, and exhibitions, in various fields. With a diverse range of activities, Persona Fest 2023 promises to be an unforgettable experience for all attendees.
